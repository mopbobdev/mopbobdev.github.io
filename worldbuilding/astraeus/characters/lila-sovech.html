Early Life

Lila was born on Sovara in 3324 AE. Her parents, Bora and Soya Sovech, were scientists affiliated with the Proteus Project and conducted genetic research alongside Dad Lorano and Mirela Lorano. Born as part of an experimental programme for the Proteus Project, Lila was genetically modified with colour changing traits derived from the Zova, an apex predator species native to Sovara. She spent her early childhood on Sovara, where she grew up alongside Ari Lorano, the son of her parents' work partners who was also genetically modified as part of the Proteus Project. During this period, the pair were frequently cared for by her grandmother while their parents were working.

In 3327 AE, when Lila was three years old, a containment breach involving a pair of Zova resulted in the destruction of the research laboratory and the death of her parents. Following the incident, Ari’s family fled Sovara, leaving Lila alone with her grandmother.

Growing up

Lila's village, who already didn't trust humans, blamed the humans for the containment breach, and Lila grew to despise all humans. As Lila grew older she was taught to hunt, a major part of the Sovari culture.
