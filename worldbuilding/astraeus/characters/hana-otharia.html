Bibliography

Hana was born and raised on Acropolis. She was born into a rich family, with her father, Kuda Torenai, being the CEO of Omega Tech.

She had been artistic since a child.

Her father sent her to boarding school, she didn't like school. She would skip classes and sneak out at night.

She had a hideout on the top of an abandoned building in the outskirts of the city that she would take the bus to each night.
