Lost

Lila slowly wakes up. Shes on the cold uneven ground. Her body aches. It feels heavy and sore, her feathers are matted and her face is puffy from crying. She has bruises and scrapes all over her body. For a moment she doesn't recognise where she is, its still slightly dark, dawn light filtering through the canopy.

She hears someone breathing beside her. She looks up.

Ari is asleep, half slumped against a tree.

He looks exhausted, bruised from when she threw him, arms scraped from running through the forest, hair sticking everywhere.

This is the moment she realises something quietly devastating: He stayed. Even after what I did.

Fragments of memory slam into her all at once.

Flashes of white light.
Bones crunching against themselves.
Muscles twisting and spasming.
Wings snapping out, hitting trees.
Her vision strobing with flashing feathers.
The crushing pressure inside her skull like it was trying to split open.
Her own screams, echoing in the dark.

She remembers wanting it to stop.
She remembers being sure she was going to kill someone.

And then she remembers Ari. A memory of his hands pinning her arms. His voice telling her to trust him. His touch easing something inside her no one has ever been able to reach. She doesn’t understand how that’s possible.

Her chest tightens.

Lila processes what Ari did. This is where the seeds of deeper emotion start. She sees him differently now. Gentle instead of chaotic. Brave instead of reckless. Familiar instead of strange

And also: Why didn’t he run when I told him to? Why stay with a monster? Her self-hatred surfaces, but soft and confused, not dramatic. Maybe she picks a leaf out of his hair without waking him, then freezes like why did I just do that? - Very subtle affection, not overt romance.

What is Ari? What does the agency want with them both?

Lila checks over his injuries. She realises how much he pushed himself to reach her.

“…thank you,” she whispers, barely audible.

She tries to stand but her knees buckle immediately. A sharp ache shoots up her wings. She collapses back down beside him. For a moment, she leans against his shoulder. (Not intentionally intimate, just because shole needs something solid to keep herself upright.)

Ari groans and Lila jerks away from him.

He stirs, confused, eyes half-open. He rubs his eyes.

“…Good morning,” she says quietly.

Ari blinks at her, startled, almost like he wasn’t expecting to see her there.

“Morning,” he mutters, stretching his back His spine clicks. “Ugh, what happened? I feel terrible.” (He doesn’t remember anything clearly when he uses his ability too much.)

Lila hesitates.

She watches him get up, heart thudding, wondering whether she should tell him everything… or keep the worst of it to herself.

"Ari... How did you do that?"

"Do what?"

He really doesn't remember.

"...never mind"

Ari holds out his hand, offering to pull Lila up. She takes it and is pulled of the ground.

"Where are we?" Ari started walking off into the forest in a random direction.

Suddenly Lila realises they don't know their way back.

Maybe they are looking for the way out. Then realise they probably should have just stayed where they were, and realise they are probably deeper into the forest than before.
